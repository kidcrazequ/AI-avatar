## 1 前言 Foreword

本规范中所有的技术数据和要求需要部件供应商无条件地遵守，部件供应商不仅要遵循技术规范中的
要求，而且需通过试验来验证自己的产品。所有不符合该规范的偏差都必须由供应商详细列出，偏差只
有在得到远景能源技术部门的书面批准后才会被接受，任何口头的协议将不会被认可。
Supplier and his supplied products must unconditionally and without any reservation comply with all
requirements set forth in this specification. Deviations from this specification must explicitly be disclosed
and transmitted to Envision R&D department by supervisor. Thereby approved by the R&D, Commercial
and Legal departments of Envision Energy as written approvals. Oral or verbal agreements are not
acceptable by any means.

## 2 适用范围 Scope

本规范适用于远景能源有限公司（以下简称远景能源）风力发电机组系统（部件）的采购、制造、测
试、检验，以及交付的过程要求。
This specification applies for machining, manufacturing, transportation and purchasing of wind
turbine components for Envision Energy Megawatt Wind Turbine Generator (WTG).
同时在遵循各国相关包装法规前提下，为了以下目的，特制定本规范
Meanwhile under the premise of complying with the relevant packaging regulations of various
countries, formulate this technical specification for the following purposes：
• 规范各类零部件包装方式
Standardize the packaging methods of various parts；
• 满足产品在装卸,运输以及不同环境中的仓储过程中的防护需求
Meet the storage protection requirements of products in different environments such as loading,
unloading and transportation；
• 为了推动包装的标准化和可持续发展
Promote the standardization and sustainable development of packaging。
本规范可作为远景能源质量管理人员判定发货的采购件来料包装是否合格，以及判定市场投诉责任
单位的主要依据之一
This specification can be used as one of the main reference document for Envision Energy's quality management
personnel to determine whether the packaging of purchased parts is qualified, and to determine the responsible
unit for market complaints。

## 5.1.2 热收缩膜 Heat Shrink Film

热收缩膜是一种广泛应用于包装行业的材料，特别是用于商品的集合包装和保护。它可以根据需要
收缩，紧密地包裹住产品以达到稳固、遮盖和保护产品的目的。
Heat shrink film is a material widely used in the packaging industry, especially for collective
packaging and protection of goods. Heat shrink film can shrink as needed to wrap the product tightly for
stabilizing, covering and protecting the product.
热收缩膜可由多种材料制成，包括聚乙烯（PE）、聚氯乙烯（PVC）、聚丙烯（PP）和 PET（聚对
苯二甲酸乙二醇酯）等。不同材料的选择会影响膜的性能，如收缩率、耐热性和耐撕裂性。远景热收缩
膜的要求遵循标准 GB/T 13519。
Heat shrink film can be made from a variety of materials, including polyethylene (PE), polyvinyl
chloride (PVC), polypropylene (PP) and polyethylene terephthalate (PET). The different materials will
affect the performance of the film, such as shrinkage, heat resistance and tear resistance. The
requirements for Envision heat shrink film follow the standard GB/T 13519.
热收缩膜必须具有一定的耐穿刺性，良好的收缩性和一定的收缩应力。在收缩过程中，薄膜不能产
生孔洞。由于收缩膜经常适用于室外，因此需要加入 UV 抗紫外线剂。
The heat shrink film must have a specified puncture resistance, good shrinkage and a specified
shrinkage stress. During the shrinking process, the film cannot produce holes. Since heat shrink films are
often used outdoors, UV stabilizers require to be added.

## 5.2.2 木质包装结构类型 Wooden packaging structure types

从结构上，木质包装主要包含木箱和木托盘形式。针对不同的装卸方式需要满足相应的设计要求
Structurally, wooden packaging mainly includes wooden boxes and wooden pallets. Different loading and
unloading methods need to meet corresponding design requirements：
• 吊运设计 Lifting design：
使用木箱包装时，需根据货物整体的长度、重量、重心情况合理设计起吊位置，并标明吊点。使用
木托盘包装时，应优先考虑在部件上设置吊钩、吊点。
When using wooden boxes for packaging, the lifting position must be reasonably designed
according to the overall length, weight, and center of gravity of the goods, and the lifting points must be
marked. When using wooden pallets for packaging, priority should be given to setting lifting lugs and
points on the components.
起吊或运输固定时，通过这些吊点进行部件的吊装与运输中的固定，另外可以根据货物的重量在起
吊位置可以适当加装相应规格的护角铁板，以增加强度。
When lifting or fixing during transportation, these lifting points are used to lift the components and fix
them during transportation. In addition, according to the weight of the goods, angle iron plates of
corresponding specifications can be installed at the lifting position to increase the strength.
• 叉运设计 Forklift design：
为了满足各类叉车装卸作业，木箱、木托盘底部与地面的空隙高度 H 必须大于 95 mm，左右两木墩

height H between the bottom of wooden boxes and wooden pallets and the ground must be greater than

金属包装指的是使用金属材料（如铝、钢、锡等）制作的包装器具。远景常用的金属包装形式主要
是金属料架，针对此类包装的材质，需要满足以下要求 Metal packaging is made of metal materials (such
as aluminum, steel, tin and so on). The metal packaging form commonly used by Envision is mainly metal
racks. The material of this type of packaging requires to meet the following requirements：
• 金属包装本身需要具有良好的防腐蚀性能。需要基于应用场景选择相应的材料和表面处理工艺，
避免因包装的腐蚀将腐蚀传递到产品或造成产品在装卸、运输和存储过程中损坏。
The metal packaging requires to have good anti-corrosion properties. Selection of materials and
surface treatment processes is based on the application scenarios to avoid corrosion of the
packaging from being transferred to the product or causing damage to the product during loading,
unloading, transportation and storage.
• 金属包装需要有足够的强度和物理性能，能够在运输和储存过程中提供足够的保护，防止产品
损伤。如金属料架方管的弯曲度不得大于 2mm/m，总弯曲度不得大于总长度的 0.2%。
Metal packaging requires to have sufficient strength and physical properties to provide adequate
protection during transportation and storage. For example, the curvature of the square tube of
the metal rack must not be greater than 2 mm/m, and the total curvature must not be greater
than 0.2 % of the total length.
• 金属包装材料不应与包装的产品发生化学反应。

## 5.3.3 塑料包装材料要求 Plastic packaging material requirements

## 6 包装结构设计 Packaging structure design

在进行包装结构设计时，应考虑到产品尺寸、重量和形状特征进行布局规划，同时遵循以下原则
When designing the packaging structure, the product size, weight and shape characteristics must be
considered for layout planning, and the following principles should be followed：

---

## 1) 满足包装设计要求 Packaging design requirements：

包装设计应符合国内外的包装和运输安全和法规要求，保障货物在包装和运输过程中的安全和合法
性。包装本身重心要均衡， 满足吊装、搬运、存储要求，避免倾覆。
The packaging design must comply with domestic and international packaging and transportation
safety and regulatory requirements to ensure the safety and legality of the goods during packaging and
transportation. The center of gravity of the packaging itself must be balanced to meet the requirements
of lifting, handling and storage to avoid overturning.
• 保护性功能 Protective function：
产品包装应能够有效地保护产品免受损坏和污染，确保产品在运输和储存过程中的完整性和质量。
例如外包装需设置足够强度的固定绑扎固定点，使运输过程中有效固定包装且不会发生包装损坏。
Product packaging must be able to effectively protect the product from damage and contamination,
and ensure the integrity and quality of the product during transportation and storage. For example, the
outer packaging must be equipped with fixed lashing points of sufficient strength to effectively fix the
packaging during transportation and prevent the packaging from being damaged.

---

## 2) 方便使用 Convenient to use：

• 设计的包装应考虑整个过程的便捷性。即方便包装操作，方便堆码，方便装卸，方便运输和储
存以及方便拆包操作。
• The packaging must be designed considering the convenience of the entire process. The
packaging design must be convenient for packaging operation, stacking, loading and unloading,
transportation and storage, and unpacking operation.

---

## 3) 有效的空间利用 Efficient space utilization：

• 在保证产品包装的保护功能和便捷性的前提下，结构设计时应进行合理的布局规划，充分利用
空间来提升包装的容积率。
• Under the premise of ensuring the protection function and convenience of product packaging,
reasonable layout planning must be carried out during structural design to make full use of space
to increase the volume ratio of packaging.

---

## 4) 成本控制 Cost Control：

• 在满足上述功能的基础上，还需考虑成本控制，通过材料选择、结构简化等手段，实现成本的
有效控制，确保包装的经济性。
Under the premise of meeting the above requirements, cost control must be considered.
Through means such as material selection and structural simplification, effective cost control of
packaging can be achieved.

## 7 包装防护要求 Packaging protection requirements

为了应对外界环境压力，产品的包装在全周期内需要对内部产品提供一定的防护功能。供应商应负
责全周期内的包装防护质量，包括从供应商发运至现场装配前所经受的外界自然环境。
To cope with external environmental pressure, product packaging must provide certain protection
functions for the internal products throughout the entire life cycle. The supplier shall be responsible for
the quality of packaging protection throughout the entire cycle, including the external natural environment
from the shipment to on-site assembly.
供应商应基于研发对产品的防护要求而采取对应的包装防护等级并且体现在包装方案的设计中，同
时包装设计要考虑到物流装卸，日常维护，海关查验等环节对包装的开口和复用要求，包装的保护性不
应由于以上环节而损失功能。
Suppliers should adopt corresponding packaging protection levels based on the protection
requirements of R&D for products and reflect them in the design of packaging solutions. At the same time,
packaging design must consider the opening and reuse requirements of packaging in logistics loading
and unloading, daily maintenance, customs inspection and other process. The protectiveness of the
packaging cannot be compromised due to the above process.

## 9 包装数量要求 Loading quantity requirements

每个产品的包装数量定义应当遵循以下原则 The definition of packaging quantity for each product
should follow the principles below：

---

## 1) 包装数量的定义应考虑包装容器的尺寸要求，形成发货包装的尺寸要能够尽量最大利用货车和货

柜的空间以提高运输装载率。
The definition of packaging quantity should consider the size requirements of the packaging
container, and the size of the shipping package should be able to maximize the use of truck and
container space to increase the transportation loading rate.

---

## 2) 产品包装数量应设定为整机用量的约数或倍数以避免零头。

The product packaging quantity should be set as an approximation or multiple of the entire
machine dosage to avoid odds.

---

## 3) 同时产品的包装单元数量设定应当基于零件的需求量和发货频次，满足库存最小原则，避免产生

呆滞物料。
At the same time, the number of packaging units for the product should be set based on the
demand for parts and the frequency of delivery to meet the principle of minimum inventory and
avoid the generation of obsolete inventory.

## 10 环保和可持续要求 Environmental protection and sustainable

requirements
为了减少对环境的负面影响，并确保资源的有效利用，供应商在包装方案的设计和开发过程应当遵
循以下原则 To reduce negative impacts on the environment and ensure efficient use of resources,
suppliers should follow the following principles in the design and development of packaging solutions：

---

## 1) 包装减量化,在满足产品防护，运输仓储等包装功能的条件下，尽量少的使用包装材料。

Packaging reduction: Use as little packaging materials as possible while meeting packaging
functions such as product protection, transportation and warehousing.

---

## 2) 鼓励将包装设计成可以多次使用的形式以减少包装废物的产生。

Tend to design packaging that it can be used multiple times to reduce the generation of
packaging waste.

---

## 3) 优先选择可循环材料的包装，确保包装在使用后可以被回收利用。

Give priority to packaging made of recyclable materials and ensure that the packaging can be
recycled after use.

---

## 4) 包装材料使用能在自然环境中生物降解的材料，以确保包装材料在废弃后不会对环境造成长期污

染。
The packaging materials used are biodegradable in the natural environment to ensure that the
packaging materials will not cause long-term pollution to the environment after being discarded.

---

## 5) 避免使用含有害化学物质或者有害物质超标的包装，以减少对环境和人体健康的影响，如包材需

要符合 RoHS 标准,胶合板需使用等级达到 E2 级以上环保板材。
Avoid using packaging containing harmful chemicals or excessive amounts of harmful
substances to reduce the impact on the environment and human health. For example, packaging
materials must comply with RoHS standards, and plywood must use environmentally friendly
boards that are E2 or above.

---

for package

## 13 其它特殊包装要求 Other special packaging requirements

13 其它特殊包装要求 Other special packaging requirements

---

## 1) 同一物料号的零件包括附件应放入同一包装内。

Parts including accessories with the same material number must be placed in the same package.

---

## 2) 如附件较大或者商品编码不同无法放入同一包装,需与远景能源相应零件设计团队沟通，经过评

审批准后才能单独包装。
If the accessories are too large or have different product codes to fit into the same package,
operator shall communicate with the corresponding parts design team of Envision Energy and
only package them separately after review and approval.

---

## 3) 包装和发货前检查，不允许有其他无关异物留存在包装或零件内外。

During the inspection before packaging and shipment, no foreign matter is allowed to remain
inside or outside the packaging or parts.

---

## 4) 发货包装的打包带应为全新料 PP/PET 材料塑钢带，厚度>1mm，宽度>15mm。纸箱和木箱应用打

包带井字形打包 (长宽方向至少各打两道)，外箱长度较长则需多打几道，相邻打包带间间距离不
超过 1m。打包带应与外箱同方向棱边平行，不要打斜打歪。
The packing tape for delivery packaging should be new PP or PET material plastic-steel tape
with a thickness of >1mm and a width of >15mm. Cardboard boxes and wooden boxes must be
packed in a cross pattern with packing tape (at least two strips in length and width). If the outer
box is longer, more strips should be added. The distance between adjacent packing tapes must
not exceed 1m. The packing tape must be parallel to the edge of the outer box in the same
direction, and not be slanted or crooked.

---

## 5) 随着市场需求和包装应用场景的增加，部分零部件需要基于特殊要求设计包装。对需要特殊包装

防护的物料，在以上条件基础上，必须同时满足与之对应设计文件的特定技术要求规定。
With the increase in market demand and packaging application scenarios, some parts need to
be packaged based on special requirements. For materials that require special packaging
protection, in addition to the above conditions, the material must also meet the specific technical
requirements of the corresponding design documents.

## 14 包装确认流程 Packaging confirmation process

14 包装确认流程 Packaging confirmation process

---

## 1) 在供应商评审阶段，供应商应基于远景的相关各项技术规范和要求制订包装方案。按照引用文件

中零件包装确认单（PAF）格式提交给远景，由远景能源批准包装方案和成本。且方案按照引用
文件中包装运输和堆码测试报告列出的项目进行包装试验通过后方可开始供货。
During the supplier review phase, suppliers can develop packaging solutions based on the
relevant technical specifications and requirements of Envision. Submit the packaging plan to
Envision with Parts Packaging Validation Form (PAF) format in Appendix 1, and the packaging
plan and cost will be approved by Envision Energy. And the supply can only start after the
packaging test is passed according to the items listed in the packaging, transportation and
stacking test report in Appendix 2.

---

## 2) 远景能源的现场 QC 人员和 QE 人员，将持续监督包装方案的执行情况，并根据现场实际需求提

出改进优化意见。
Envision Energy's on-site QC and QE personnel will continue to monitor the implementation of
the packaging solution and put forward improvement and optimization suggestions based on
actual on-site needs.

---

## 3) 对于 QC、QE 人员反馈的缺陷或改进意见，经由 PE 确认后，将向供应商提出整改意见。必要时

将要求供应商重新制包装方案、填写 PAF 及进行试验。
For defects or improvement suggestions reported by QC and QE personnel, after confirmation
by PE, Envision will provide rectification suggestions to suppliers. If necessary, the supplier will
be required to re-make the packaging plan, fill in the PAF and conduct tests.

---

## 4) 零件供应商应负责整个包装防护周期内的包装质量，即从供应商处发运起至现场装配前物流、存

储全周期内零部件质量安全，经受包括外界环境/物流装卸及检查操作/正常运输冲击等。
Suppliers must be responsible for the packaging quality throughout the entire packaging
protection cycle, that is, the parts quality and safety of logistics and storage full cycle from
supplier to on-site assembly. Parts suppliers must withstand impacts including the external
environment, logistics loading and unloading and inspection operations, normal transportation.

---

## 5) 供应商不得随意变更包装形式，任何变更应提前向远景能源提出书面申请并提交包装确认单，由

远景能源批准后方能实施。
Suppliers are not allowed to change the packaging format at will. Any changes must be submitted
in writing to Envision Energy in advance and a packaging confirmation form must be submitted.
The changes can only be implemented after approval by Envision Energy.
