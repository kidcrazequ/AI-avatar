## 7 Surface preparation 表面处理

The primary objective of surface preparation is to ensure the removal of deleterious matter and to obtain a surface
roughness that permits satisfactory adhesion between the priming paint and the substrate. It will also assist in
reducing the amount of contaminants that cause corrosion.
表面处理的主要目的是确保除去有害物，获得表面粗糙度，增加令人满意的底漆与基材间附着力，另外
会减少导致腐蚀的污染物的数量。

---

## 7.1 Work condition requirement 作业条件要求：

Welding, cutting, grinding, and heat treatment of work pieces shall be completed before sand blasting.
在喷砂开始前，工件的电焊、切割、打磨、热处理工作应当完成。
The substrate temperature of the work pieces shall always keep at least 3℃ above the dew point throughout the
whole application process, and the relative humidity must be controlled to less than 85%, condensation must be
avoided during sand blasting cleaning.
工件温度应当永远保持至少高于露点温度 3 摄氏度以上，在整个喷砂期间和随后的时间内，相对湿度必
须控制在低于 85%的条件下，必须避免在喷砂清洁期间的冷凝现象。
Lighting in the sandblasting room: more than 500LUX, is favourable for application observation and check.
喷砂房内照明：500LUX 以上，以利于施工观察和检验。
Dedusting system: qualified dedusting system can effectively remove dust, and ensure the cleanliness of the
abrasive, space surface cleanliness and working process visibility.
除尘：配置合格的除尘系统，可以有效除尘，保证磨料的清洁度、空间表面清洁度和作业过程的可视
度。

## 8.11 Requirements of paint application process 油漆施工过程要求

The sprayer should apply the coatings keeping the nozzle at a right angle to the substrate at all times, and a
combination of horizontal and vertical spray passes should be performed in order to achieve an even film
thickness.
喷漆工在施工油漆时应当始终保持枪嘴与表面的正确角度，并且为了达到均匀的膜厚，应当采用水平走
枪和垂直走枪交叉的方法施工。
When spraying, keep the paint spraying vertical to the substrate at all times, and keep the nozzle 30-50cm away
from the substrate. Use 50% overlapping application techniques to get even paint film thickness, every spraying
pass should overlap 50% of the previous pass, if the overlapping part is less than 50%, the last spraying pass will
have streak on it. Pull the trigger, move the spraying gun at a uniform velocity, and since the coating flows at a
uniform velocity, when the spraying width overlap at 50%, the paint film will cover evenly.

## 13.7 Key points and requirements of the final coating control 最终产品检测要点和要求

Check points for final inspection: top coat with uniform color gloss, no sagging, no spraying mist, no
contamination; no pinholes, no bubble, no leak spray and other defects..
完工检查要点：面漆色泽均匀，无流挂、无漆雾、无污染；无针孔、无气泡、无漏喷等缺陷。
Wed film thickness (WFT) inspection ： WFT should be measured immediately after painting application,
according to ISO 2801. Regular WFT measurement can be conducted to control the paint consumption and obtain
the proper NDFT.
湿膜厚度（WFT）检测：在涂层施工后应立即检测。按照 ISO 2801 方法执行。湿膜厚度的检测可反映油
漆用量，同时也有助于控制干膜厚度。
Dry film thickness (NDFT) inspection: NDFT measurement shall be carried out after the completion of paint and
sufficient drying of each coat applied, in accordance with SSPC PA2 or ISO2801.
干膜厚度（NDFT）的检测：干膜厚度检查应在每一道涂层施工完成并硬干后进行。检测依据 SSPC-PA2
或 ISO 2801 标准执行。
Adhesion test: The measurement of adhesion and cohesion properties of coating system is destructive and
normally performed under complaints. It is a quality endorsement testing is applicable to specified or on reference
areas and it is not a routine test. Recommended procedure for the assessment of adhesion/cohesion is based on
ISO 2409 (Cross-cutting) or ISO 4624 (Pull-off test).
附着力测试：涂层系的附着力和层间结合力的测试是一种破坏性的，通常只是发生投诉，质量认可测试
时作用于制定或参照区域，并非作为一种常规测试。检测依据 ISO 2409 或 ISO 4624 标准进行划格法和标
准拉开法执行。
Gloss requirement: the gloss of paint with high light gloss requirements must be in 50-80 GU.
光泽度要求：面漆有高光要求的光泽度必须在 50-80 GU 之间

## 16 Health, safety and environmental protection 健康、安全和环保

Do not use, whenever possible, any coating material containing lead, chromate, asphalt, bitumen or coal tar, as
well as any abrasive material considered hazardous to the health of the operators. Paint manufacturer must provide
MSDS (material safety data sheet) of all specified coating materials, including curing agents, base, solvents and
thinners. MSDS shall be available any time at the job site. MSDS and documentation should be in accordance
with EN / ISO 20340.
尽可能不用含铅、铬酸盐、柏油、沥青、煤焦油以及对工人健康有害的磨料。油漆厂商必须提供所有配
套油漆的 MSDS（化学品安全说明书），包括固化剂，基料，溶剂和稀释剂。施工现场必须确保能随时
获取 MSDS。化学品安全说明书应满足 EN / ISO 20340 的要求。
Main safety risk of painting operation：
涂装作业主要的安全风险：
● Danger of explosion or fire
爆炸或失火的危险
● Danger of suffocation by solvent fumes

## 16.2 Storage and maintenance of coating materials 油漆材料的储存和维护

储存现场要保持通风，存放的环境低温不要低于 5℃，高温不要超过 40℃。

of the contents, colour identification, production batch number and date of packaging.
所有送达现场的油漆材料必须置于未开启的出厂包装容器中，并且能被清楚的识别。包装上注有厂商名
