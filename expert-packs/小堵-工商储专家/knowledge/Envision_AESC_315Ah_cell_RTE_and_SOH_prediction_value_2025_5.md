Instructions

This document provides the State of Health (SOH) predictions and Round-Trip Efficiency (RTE) for Envision AESC energy storage-specific cells within the energy storage cabinet. The State of Health prediction serves as a critical indicator of battery degradation, reflecting the remaining capacity relative to its initial state and enabling proactive lifecycle management. The SOH forecasts prediction value is a model-dependent estimate. Actual SOH deviations may arise from dynamic real-world variables, including inconsistent temperature exposure, irregular charge/discharge patterns, mechanical stress anomalies, or electrolyte depletion, which are not fully captured in standardized testing scenarios.
