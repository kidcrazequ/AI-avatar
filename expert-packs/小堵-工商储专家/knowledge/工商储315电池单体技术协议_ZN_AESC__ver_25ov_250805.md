在（25±2）℃下，电池充放电容量及能量试验按照下列步骤进行：

a） 0.5P恒功率放电到2.5V，静置30min；

b） 0.5P恒功率充电到3.65V，静置2min，0.1P恒功率充电到3.65V，静置30min；

c） 0.5P恒功率放电到2.5V，静置2min，0.1P恒功率放电到2.5V，静置30 min；

d） 重复步骤b）～c）3次，以3次试验的均值作为结果，记录电池容量、能量数据。

Capacity and energy test method (The test shall be performed at the cell level): (0.5P - 504W)

At (25±2) ℃, cell charge/discharge capacity and energy tests are carried out according to the following steps:

a) 0.5P constant power discharge to 2.5V, rest 30 mins.

b) 0.5P constant power charge to 3.65V, rest 2min, 0.1P constant power charge to 3.65V, rest 30min.

c) 0.5P constant power discharge to 2.5V, rest 2min, 0.1P constant power discharge to .5V, rest 30 min.

d) Repeat steps b) to c) 3 times and record the cell capacity and energy data with the mean value of the three tests as the result.

第6章 注意事项

Chapter 6 Precautions

6.1电池使用

电池成组预紧力50~800kgf(1kgf=9.8N)对电池性能无影响，电池大面平整受力挤压，不允许有尖锐或不平整面对电池进行挤压。成组激光焊接要求如下：焊接熔深0.5~1.5mm、极柱承受最大压力500N（最大垂直力，不发生变形）、极柱承受的最大温度200℃，持续时间不超过30s(塑胶垫不发生变形)。

The pre-tightening force of the cell group is 50~800kgf (1kgf=9.8N), which has no impact on the performance of the cell. The cell is subjected to compression on a large flat surface, and sharp or uneven surfaces are not allowed to carry out compression on the cell. The requirements for laser welding of the cell group are as follows: welding penetration 0.5~1.5mm, the maximum pressure that the pole can withstand is 500N (maximum vertical force, no deformation occurs), the maximum temperature that the pole can withstand is 200℃, and the continuous time does not exceed 30s (no deformation occurs with plastic pad).

6.2注意事项Precautions

禁止反向充电。正确连接电池的正负极，严禁反向充电。

Reverse charging is prohibited. Correctly connect the positive and negative terminals of the cell and strictly prohibit reverse charging.

禁止过放电。在电池正常使用过程中，为防止过放电，建议补电周期为 6个月, 存储SOC≥15%，存储温度-10～30℃，存储过程中电芯电压需＞3.2V。存储周期不考虑 BMS 或其他除单体电池以外的自耗电影响。

Over discharge is prohibited. During the normal use of the cell, in order to prevent over discharge, it is recommended that the recharge period be 6 months, the storage SOC≥15%, the storage temperature -10℃~30℃, and the battery voltage>3.2V during storage. The storage cycle does not take into account BMS or other consumable power effects other than individual batteries.

严禁将电池浸入水中，保存不用时，应放置于阴凉干燥的环境中。

It is strictly forbidden to immerse the cell in water. When not in use, it should be placed in a cool and dry environment.

禁止将电池放在热高温源旁边，如火、加热器等使用和留置。

It is prohibited to place the battery next to high temperature sources such as fire or heaters during use and storage.

在使用过程中，严禁将电池正负极颠倒。

During use, it is strictly forbidden to invert the positive and negative terminals of the cell.

禁止将电池丢于火或给电池加热。

It is prohibited to throw the battery into fire or heat it.

禁止用金属直接导通电池正负极。

It is prohibited to directly conduct the positive and negative terminals of the cell with metal.

禁止将电池与金属，如发夹、项链等一起运输或贮存。

It is prohibited to transport or store the cell together with metal objects such as hairpins or necklaces.

禁止敲击或抛掷、踩踏和弯折电池等。

It is prohibited to hit, throw, step on, or bend the cell.

禁止用钉子或其它利器刺穿电池。

It is prohibited to puncture the cell with nails or other sharp objects.

不要使用处于极热环境中的电池，如阳光直射或热天的车内。

Do not use the cell in extremely hot environments, such as direct sunlight or hot cars.

禁止在强静电和强磁场的地方使用。

It is prohibited to use in places with strong static electricity and strong magnetic fields.

如果电池漏液，电解液溅入到皮肤、眼睛、口、鼻等部位，应立即用大量清水冲洗，并马上送医治疗，否则会对人体造成严重伤害。

If the cell leaks and the electrolyte splashes onto the skin, eyes, mouth, nose, etc., immediately rinse with plenty of water and seek medical treatment immediately, otherwise it will cause serious harm to the human body.

如果电池出现异味、发热、变色、变形或使用、贮存、充电过程中出现任何异常时不得使用。

If the cell has any abnormal odor, heat, discoloration, deformation, or any other abnormalities during use, storage, or charging, it must not be used.
