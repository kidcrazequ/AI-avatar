EHS协议

EHS Agreement

甲方：远景能源有限公司（以下简称甲方）

PART A: ENVISION ENERGY

乙方：            公司（以下简称乙方）

PART B:                                      

项目/服务名称：                                         

Name of Project/Service:

项目/服务期限：长期

Validity of Project/Service: long-term

为明确甲方安全的相关要求，规范乙方人员在甲方作业场所服务期间或甲方人员在乙方作业场所工作期间的安全行为，确保甲、乙双方人员的人身安全，避免财产损失及环境污染，根据国家和项目当地法律法规的相关要求，经甲、乙双方友好协商一致，特签订本协议。

In order to clarify the relevant requirements of Party A's safety protection, standardize the safety behavior of Party B's personnel during the service period in Party A's workplace, ensure the personal safety of Party A and Party B's personnel, and avoid property loss and environmental pollution, this agreement is signed by and between Party A and Party B through friendly negotiation in accordance with the relevant requirements of national and local laws and regulations.

本协议需在乙方进入甲方Ariba系统时签署完成，协议效力随与甲方发生业务合作开始起至不再与甲方发生业务合作结束终止。已在甲方Ariba系统内的乙方，需与甲方签署本协议的最新版。

This agreement shall be signed when Party B enters Party A's Ariba system, and the effectiveness of the agreement shall be terminated from the beginning of business cooperation with Party A to the end of no business cooperation with Party A. Party B who has been in Party A's Ariba system shall sign the latest version of this agreement with Party A.

协议内容：

Contents of the Agreement

乙方在作业期间应遵守甲方安全相关要求，包括但不限于如下列出的要求，因违反要求造成的损失由乙方承担：

During the construction, Party B should abide by the Party A's SAFETY requirements, including but not limited to the following SAFETY requirements, and the loss caused due to the breach of the requirements should be borne by Party B.

企业资质、人员要求

Corporate qualification, personnel qualification

乙方应在取得国家有关主管部门规定的相应等级及安全资质后方可承揽相应的作业，严禁越级或无资质承揽。

Party B can contract the corresponding construction works only after obtaining the corresponding grade and safety qualification stipulated by the competent government departments, and illegal contracting is not allowed.

乙方项目经理和安全管理人员必须持有合格的安全管理资格证书；对于国家规定需要注册安全工程师的项目，需配备注册安全工程师并满足数量要求。

Project manager and safety management personnel must hold qualified safety management qualification certificate and for projects requiring registered safety engineers according to national regulations, they shall be equipped with registered safety engineers and meet the quantity requirements.

乙方应遵守国家关于对工程分包和劳务分包的要求，包括但不限于禁止转包、禁止违法分包、禁止二次分包。

Party B shall comply with the national requirements on project subcontracting and labor subcontracting, including but not limited to prohibition of subcontracting, illegal subcontracting and secondary subcontracting.

乙方所有入场作业人员涉及到特种作业的，必须按照法律法规要求，取得应急管理部门颁发的《特种作业操作资格证》（包括但不限于高处作业、电工作业、焊接与热切割作业等）才能上岗操作。使用特种设备作业的人员必须持有质量技术监督管理部门颁发的《特种设备作业人员证》(包括但不限于起重机作业、压力容器作业等)。上述证书应按规定复审，逾期未复审视为无效。此外根据公司安全管理要求，入场作业人员还需持有红十字会颁发的急救证。

注：《特种作业操作资格证》和《特种设备作业人员证》的具体类别由EHS部根据作业实际情况确定。

All operators of Party B who are involved in special operations must obtain the special operation qualification certificate (including but not limited to working at height, electrician operation, welding and thermal cutting operation) issued by the emergency management department according to the requirements of laws and regulations. Personnel using special equipment must hold special equipment operator certificate (including but not limited to crane operation and pressure vessel operation) issued by quality and technology supervision and management department. The above certificate shall be reexamined according to the regulations, and it shall be invalid if it is not reexamined within the time limit. In addition, according to the company's SAFETY management requirements, entry operators also need to hold the first aid certificate issued by the Red Cross.

Note: the specific categories of special operation qualification certificate and special equipment operator certificate shall be determined by EHS department according to the actual operation situation.

乙方必须组织员工进行三级安全教育培训，并完成远景的安全培训并考核通过。

The employees must accept three-level safety education, as well complete Part A’s SAFETY training and pass the examination.

乙方必须为员工购买工伤保险和额度不低于100万/人的意外伤害保险或雇主责任险（其中派驻到制造工厂的员工/供应商保险额度不低于80万），其中必须含至少20万的医疗赔偿金。

Party B should buy employment injury insurance and accident insurance for employees or no less than RMB 1,000,000 Yuan for each employee（the insurance limit of employees / suppliers dispatched to the manufacturing plant shall not be less than RMB 800,000 Yuan），including at least RMB 200,000 Yuan medical compensation.

乙方应组织员工进行岗前职业健康体检及每年度的在岗期间职业健康体检（包括但不限于电工作业、高空作业、高原作业和高温作业），见附件，确保派驻到现场的人员没有涉及相关的禁忌症，并将汇总结果提交甲方。乙方还应要求派驻到甲方作业现场的人员无影响工作的重大疾病（如癫痫、心脏病、冠心病、高血压、心脑血管疾病、肺结核、糖尿病、精神病、呼吸系统传染病）。

Party B should organize pre-job and annual occupational physical examination for employees((including but not limited to aerial work, low voltage operation, work at plateau, High-temperature operation) (Appendix), ensure there is no related contraindications for the employees, the occupational physical condition should inform to Part A . Party B shall also require the personnel dispatched to Party A's operation site to have no major diseases (such as epilepsy, heart disease, coronary heart disease, hypertension, cardiovascular and cerebrovascular diseases, tuberculosis, diabetes, psychosis, respiratory infectious diseases) affecting the work.

乙方所派人员具备独立承担作业的能力，熟悉作业工艺流程，熟悉作业风险及安全要求，具备作业安全风险辨识和规避能力。

The assigned personnel should be able to operate independently, be familiar with operation process and procedure, operation risks and safety requirements, and have the ability to identify and avoid operation safety risks.

现场作业安全要求

Safety requirements

乙方必须确保进入到乙方或乙方负责的现场的甲方人员安全，包括但不限于遵守乙方自己的安全管理规定、甲方工作时有乙方专业人员陪同、暂停高风险作业等确保甲方人员安全的措施。

Party B must ensure the safety of Party A's personnel entering Party B or the site under Party B's responsibility, including but not limited to complying with Party B's own safety management regulations, accompanied by Party B's professional personnel when Party A works, suspension of high-risk operations and other measures to ensure the safety of Party A's personnel.

通用要求：

General requirements：

乙方应建立安全相关管理标准，保证岗位风险评估、三级安全教育、现场监督检查等符合甲方标准要求。

Party B shall establish relevant SAFETY management standards to ensure that job risk assessment, three-level safety education, on-site supervision and inspection meet the requirements of Party A's standards.

进入甲方作业现场前，乙方应将人员信息、资质证件、使用施工机具和化学品以及作业方案等报送甲方审批。甲方的审批不免除乙方对以上材料的责任。

Party B shall carry out dangerous operations, such as hoisting, aerial work, road transport, road rehabilitation, hot work and irregular operation according to the operation scheme provided by Party A。Party A's approval does not exempt Party B's the responsibility for the above materials

乙方提交的作业方案必须与实际作业内容相符，识别作业过程的安全风险并采取相应控制措施。需要专家评审的作业方案应提交专家评审。

The operation plan submitted by Party B must be consistent with the actual operation content, identify the safety risks in the operation process and take corresponding control measures. The operation scheme requiring expert review shall be submitted to experts for review.

施工作业期间，乙方每天作业必须执行远景现场的安全许可制度。

During the construction and operation period, safety license for operation should be applied on site every day.

乙方应按照甲方要求，自行向作业人员提供劳动防护用品，包括但不限于风电专用全身式安全带，与所服务现场防坠系统匹配的防坠器，圆绳、带缓冲节、金属钩可以挂进塔筒爬梯侧扶手的双钩安全绳，安全帽（不脏污，30个月以内），防砸/防穿刺/1000V绝缘安全鞋，PPE专用背包等（删除），甲方不负责提供任何个人防护用品、工具、设备；乙方负责自行检查提供给现场作业人员的PPE并须有检查合格标签可查，以确保所有PPE均受控。

Party B should provide operators with labor protection appliance according to the requirements of Party A, including but not limited to dedicated full body harness for wind power, slider matched with the fall protection system in the wind turbine, round strand wire rope, double-hook safety rope with buffer knot and metal hook which can be hanged on the handrail of the cat ladder in the tower, helmet (not stained within 30 months), smash-proof/ puncture-proof/1,000V safety (insulation) shoes, dedicated PPE knapsack, insulation and flashover-proof appliance which meets the corresponding electrical protection, etc., and Party A shall not be responsible for providing any personal protective articles, tools or devices. All PPE provided by Party B shall be equipped with “check pass” label, Party B take their own responsible for inspecting all PPEs used on-site.

作业现场机械严禁超载和带病运行，运行中禁止维护保养。操作人员离机或作业中停电时，必须切断电源。

Overload or running with problems on the construction site is not allowed, and the maintenance during running is not allowed. Power should be cut off if operator leaves the machine or outage occurs during operation.

车辆及驾驶要求：

Vehicle and driving requirements

乙方驾驶车辆进入甲方现场为自发行为，承担一切责任。甲方保留对乙方车辆在风场范围内违规处罚的权利。

Party B shall take whole responsibility of the vehicle safety management, and Party A keep the right to punish Party B if violation happens.

平原风场可以使用两驱越野或皮卡，非平原风场须为四驱越野或皮卡。允许卡车入场，卡车不强制要求四驱。

2WD off-road vehicle or truck can be used in plain wind farm, while 4WD off-road vehicle or truck is required for non-plain wind field. Trucks are allowed to enter the site, and trucks are not required with 4WD.

如山地风场路况较好，可用两驱车，可在供应商入场前由DQE向本部门负责人邮件审批。 

If the road condition of non-plain wind farm is good, 2WD vehicles can be used, and DQE can send an email to the department head for approval before the supplier enters the site.

对于特定条件下无法满足四驱车和保险的情况， 需得到远景DQE部门负责人同意审批邮件，项目现场依据同意邮件准予供应商车辆入场，否则禁止入场。 

If the 4WD vehicle and insurance can not be met under specific conditions, the approval email from the head of DQE department shall be obtained, and the supplier's vehicle shall be allowed to enter the site according to the consent email, otherwise, the access is prohibited.

车辆证照齐全、合法有效且通过年检；

All vehicles shall have complete set of license and pass the annual check.

保险满足要求，包括但不限于：机动车交通事故责任强制保险、机动车损失险、第三者责任险（不低于100万）； 

Requirements of insurance, including but not limit to: Compulsory Traffic Accident Liability Insurance for Motor Vehicles, Damage insurance, Third Party Liability Insurance (≥1,000,000RMB);

乙方租用车辆需保证车龄7年以内或行车里程不超过30万公里。

Party B shall guarantee that the vehicle age is less than 7 years or the driving mileage shall not exceed 300000 km。

车辆所有设施均完好无损；

All facilities are in good condition;

安全配置包含但不限于：安全气囊、防抱死系统（前后）、车载灭火器、常用维修工具、反光三角警示、备胎、行车记录仪（具备自动覆盖功能）；

Safety equipment, including but not limit to: air bag, ABS, fire extinguisher, common maintenance tools, warning signs, spare tyre, automobile data recorder, etc.

电气安全要求：

Electrical safety

乙方必须按《临时用电安全技术规范》（JGJ46）规定编写作业现场临时用电组织设计，并在审核合格后实施。

Party B must draft organization and design for temporary electricity on construction site according to Technical Code for Safety of Temporary Electrification on Construction Site (JGJ46) and implement after being reviewed and approved.

作业现场临时用电必须采用TN-S系统，符合“三级配电逐级保护”，达到“一机、一箱、一闸、一漏”的要求。

For temporary electricity on construction site, TN-S system should be adopted which is consistent with “stage-by-stage protection for three-stage power distribution” and reaches the requirement of “one electric device, one distribution box, one knife switch and one leakage protector”.

配电箱设置、线路敷设、接零保护、接地装置、电气联接、漏电保护等各种配电装置应符合《施工现场临时用电安全技术规范》（JGJ46）要求。

All power distribution devices, such as distribution box setting, wiring, neutral protection, earthing device, electrical connection, and leakage protection, should be consistent with Technical Code for Safety of Temporary Electrification on Construction Site (JGJ46).

配电箱、开关箱及其电器配件必须使用合格产品，应标明编号、分路标记、用途，箱门应完好并配有门锁，由专人负责管理。

Distribution box, switch box and other electric appliances should be qualified products with clear numbers, shunt circuit marks, and usage, the door should be in good condition and equipped with lock, and special personnel should be assigned to manage.

配电箱的各控制回路应标明所控制的设备名称。保护接零和工作接零的端子应分隔设置，并作明显标识。

All control circuits of the distribution box should be marked with the names of the control devices. The terminals for protective connection to neutral and working connection to neutral should be set separately and marked clearly.

作业现场使用的电气设备必须符合防火要求，严禁超负荷使用电气设备。临时用电必须安装过载保护装置，配电箱内不准使用易燃、可燃材料。

The electrical devices used on the construction site should be consistent with fire control requirements. Overload operation is not allowed. Overload protection device should be installed for temporary power, and flammable and combustible materials are not allowed in the distribution box.

电气作业必须穿戴相应的绝缘、防弧闪保护用品，并经过断电、验电后方可作业。

Electrical work should be started with insulated flash-proof articles after outage and verification is done.

乙方作业人员现场作业必须严格遵守远景电气作业程序及防护要求。

Party B must follow all Envision working instructions and protection requirements of electrical operation on site.

消防安全要求：

Fire safety

在作业现场从事明火作业必须办理动火证，清除或隔离可燃物，作业现场要有明显的消防安全标志，指定防火负责人和监护人，按规定选用和配备各类型、规格的灭火器材。

Personnel who carry out hot work on construction site should have certificate for hot work, remove or isolate combustible materials, the construction site should have obvious fire safety signs, fire control personnel and guardian should be assigned, fire extinguishing appliance of different types and specifications should be selected and equipped.

施工管理区域内严禁吸烟、明火取暖，不得私拉乱接电气线路或使用大功率电器设备。

Smoking, heating with open fire, power connection without permission or using high-power electrical appliance within the construction management area are not allowed.

进入风机内严禁抽烟。

Smoking in wind turbine generator system is not allowed.

起重吊装应要求：

Hoisting work

吊车选择应满足：吊车负荷率≤90%；吊车臂杆与吊物外轮廓最小安全距离≥0.5m；吊钩顶部滑轮组中心距吊钩底部最小安全距离≥7m。

Crane selection shall meet the following requirements: crane load rate ≤ 90%; The minimum safe distance between the crane arm and the external contour of the object is ≥ 0.5m; The minimum safe distance between the center of the pulley block at the top of the hook and the bottom of the hook is ≥ 7m.

设备倒运、吊车组拆车时严格执行施工方案和安全措施。

The construction scheme and safety measures should be implemented strictly to carry equipment or install crawler crane.

起重吊装作业前应划定作业区域，设置醒目的警示标志和现场监护人员。

A construction area should be delimited before hoisting work, attractive warning signs should be set and site supervisors should be assigned.

起重吊装前要进行100%的安全检查，签字许可后方可实施吊装作业。吊装过程中安全管理人员要进行100%的全程旁站监督。

Before hoisting can be started after safety check is done thoroughly and approved. During the hoisting work, safety management personnel should carry out whole-course monitoring.

乙方作业人员现场作业必须严格遵守远景吊装作业的其他相关要求。

Party B must follow all Envision hoisting instructions on site.

严格执行关于起重吊装“十不吊”管理要求：

Strictly implement “no hoisting under below 10 circumstances” management requirements:

安全装置不灵敏、不齐全、吊车未支稳、未铺垫路基板（枕木）、未安装视频监控设备或其不能正常使用、吊车带病时不吊；

Do not hoist if the safety devices are not sensitive or complete, or the crane is not stable, or subgrade plate (sleeper) is not paved, or video monitoring device is not installed, or video monitoring device is not in working order, or the crane has trouble;

无报审批准的吊装方案、无安全和技术交底、无有效审批作业票、指挥信号不明确时不吊；

Do not hoist if there is no approved hoisting scheme, safety or technical disclosure, effective work permission, or the command signal is ambiguous;

特殊工种作业人员未持有效证照上岗、作业时无监理和专职安全员旁站、无专人司索指挥、作业区没有隔离、没有划定警戒范围时不吊；

Do not hoist if special work operators do not have effective certificate, the supervisor and full-time safety personnel are not there, there is no dedicated commander, the operation area is not equipped with separation facilities, or alert area is not delimited;

捆绑、吊挂不牢或不平衡，可能引起滑动、被吊物上有人或浮置物、容器内装的物品过满时不吊；

Do not hoist if the hoisting objects are not bound or hanged stably, firmly or in balance, the hoisting objects may slide, there is personnel or floating object on the hoisting objects, or the vessel is loaded too much;

结构或吊装零部件有影响安全缺陷或损伤时不吊；

Do not hoist if the crane structure or components have defect which may affect safety;

违反方案（作业指导书）要求时、超出吊车工作性能时不吊；

Do not hoist if it is out of the hoisting scheme (standard operation procedure) or the performance of the crane;

作业场所气象条件不满足要求,作业时风速超规程规定、有暴雨雷击风险、能见度不足无法看清场地、被吊物和指挥信号时不吊；

Do not hoist if the meteorological condition does not meet the requirement, the wind speed is out of the requirements, there is the risk of rainstorm or lightning, or the ground, hoisting object and command signal cannot be seen clearly;

被吊物棱角处与捆绑钢绳间未加衬垫保护时不吊；

Do not hoist if there is no liner between the edges and corners of the hoisting object and wire rope;

歪拉斜吊重物、超载、被吊物重量不清、遇有拉力不明的埋置物件时不吊；

Do not hoist if the hoisting object is drawn obliquely, the hoisting object is overload, or the hoisting object is pulled by unknown object;

作业时遇到任何人发出危险信号，隐患未排除前不吊。

Do not hoist if anyone gives out danger signal and the hidden danger risk is not extinguished.

化学品要求：

Chemical management

使用的化学品的MSDS信息资料应放置在作业现场，作业员应清楚并按其要求执行。

MSDS of chemicals used should be placed on the work site, and the operator should use them as required.

作业员必须遵守甲方的化学品使用管理规定。

Operators should abide by Party A's regulations on the use and management of chemicals.

化学品必须保持干燥、阴凉、通风、避免阳光直接照射。

Chemicals must be stored in dry, cool, ventilated and free from direct sunlight places.

卫生防疫要求：

Hygiene and epidemic prevention

作业现场炊事人员必须具备身体健康证。做好食品、原料的进货管理。生熟食品必须分开加工和保管，存放食品必须有遮盖。

The Part B’s kitchen on the construction site should have health certificate, the supply of foods and raw materials should be enhanced, uncooked and cooked foods should be processed and kept separately, and stored foods should be covered.

施工生活区厕所墙壁屋顶严密，门窗齐全，采用硬化地面，要有灭蝇措施，设专人负责定期保洁。

The toilets in the living quarters should have tight wall and roof, doors and windows, harden ground, and mosquito and fly eradication measures, and cleaning should be done periodically by designated personnel.

作业现场应保证供应卫生饮水，有固定的盛水容器和有专人管理，并定期清洗消毒。

The construction site should supply potable water and have fixed vessels to retain water. Management should be done by designated personnel. And cleaning and disinfection should be done periodically.

办公区、生活区应保持整洁卫生，生活垃圾与施工垃圾不得混放。垃圾应存放在密闭式容器，定期灭蝇，及时清运。
