为明确甲方安全的相关要求，规范乙方人员在甲方作业场所服务期间的安全行为，确保甲、乙双方人员的人身安全，避免财产损失及环境污染，根据国家和项目当地法律法规的相关要求，经甲、乙双方友好协商一致，特签订本协议。

In order to clarify the relevant requirements of Party A's safety protection, standardize the safety behavior of Party B's personnel during the service period in Party A's workplace, ensure the personal safety of Party A and Party B's personnel, and avoid property loss and environmental pollution, this agreement is signed by and between Party A and Party B through friendly negotiation in accordance with the relevant requirements of national and local laws and regulations

本协议需在乙方进入甲方Ariba系统时签署完成，协议效力随与甲方发生业务合作开始起至不再与甲方发生业务合作结束终止。已在甲方Ariba系统内的乙方，需与甲方签署本协议的最新版。

This agreement shall be signed when Party B enters Party A's Ariba system, and the effectiveness of the agreement shall be from the beginning of business cooperation with Party A to the end of business cooperation with Party A. Party B who has been in Party A's Ariba system shall sign the latest version of this agreement with Party A.

本协议适用于为远景提供物流运输的供应商。

This Agreement applies to suppliers who provide transportation。

协议内容：

Contents of the Agreement

乙方在为甲方提供物流运输活动期间应遵守以下安全相关要求，包括但不限于如下列出的要求，因违反要求造成的损失由乙方承担：

During transportation activities, Party B should abide by the Party A's SAFETY requirements, including but not limited to the following SAFETY requirements, and the loss caused due to the breach of the requirements should be borne by Party B.

企业资质、人员要求

Corporate qualification, personnel qualification

乙方应在取得国家有关主管部门规定的相应等级及安全资质后方可承揽相应的作业，严禁越级或无资质承揽。

Party B can contract the corresponding construction works only after obtaining the corresponding grade and safety qualification stipulated by the competent government departments, and illegal contracting is not allowed.

乙方必须组织员工进行三级安全教育培训。

The employees must accept three-level safety education.

乙方所派人员具备独立承担作业的能力，熟悉作业工艺流程，熟悉作业风险及安全要求，具备作业安全风险辨识和规避能力。

The assigned personnel should be able to operate independently, be familiar with operation process and procedure, operation risks and safety requirements, and have the ability to identify and avoid operation safety risks.

物流司机大件安全驾驶培训完成率100%。

100% completion rate of large and safety driving training for logistics drivers.

现场作业安全要求

Safety requirements

乙方现场作业必须遵守甲方安全相关要求，包括但不限于以下要求：

Party B must comply with Party A's SAFETY requirements, including but not limited to the following requirements：

乙方在提供物流服务中，应有有效防止粉尘、噪声、废气排放、废物污染的措施，如有因乙方作业过程中产生的以上排放对甲方以及周围居民的正常生活造成影响、引起纠纷的，乙方应对其负全部责任。

During the transportation activities, Party B should carry out countermeasures to prevent dust, noise, waste gas and waste pollution. If a dispute is resulted from the above discharges, Party B should take full responsibilities.

乙方在提供物流服务中，应保护现场周围的环境，防止对其造成不应有的破坏，作业过程中产生的各种垃圾，包括生活垃圾、一般废物、危险废物由乙方负责带离甲方场区自行处理，如有物品设施等受损坏或损失，需恢复原状及功能。

During the transportation activities, Party B should protect the local environment and prevent environmental destruction, and garbage, waster, hazardous waste, etc., generated during operation should be handled by Party B. Damaged or lost articles and facilities should be recovered.

在作业区域，必须使用适当的方法和方式做好相应作业区域的安全标识和隔离。

Safety identification and isolation of construction area must be done well.

乙方驾驶小型汽车进入甲方现场为自发行为，承担一切责任。甲方保留对乙方车辆在风场范围内违规处罚的权利。

Party B shall take whole responsibility of the vehicle safety management, and Party A keep the right to punish Party B if violation happens.

乙方若需驾驶小型汽车前往远景风场，车况应符合以下要求：

Situation of cars entering in wind farm from Party B shall meet the following requirements

平原风场可以使用两驱越野或皮卡，非平原风场须为四驱越野或皮卡。允许卡车入场，卡车不强制要求四驱。

2WD off-road vehicle or truck can be used in plain wind farm, while 4WD off-road vehicle or truck is required for non-plain wind field. Trucks are allowed to enter the site, and trucks are not required with 4WD.

如山地风场路况较好，可用两驱车，可在供应商入场前由物流管理部向本部门负责人邮件审批。 

If the road condition of non-plain wind farm is good, 2WD vehicles can be used, and Logistic Dept. can send an email to the department head for approval before the supplier enters the site.

对于特定条件下无法满足四驱车和保险的情况， 需得到远景物流管理部部门负责人同意审批邮件，项目现场依据同意邮件准予供应商车辆入场，否则禁止入场。 

If the 4WD vehicle and insurance can not be met under specific conditions, the approval email from the head of Logistic department shall be obtained, and the supplier's vehicle shall be allowed to enter the site according to the consent email, otherwise, the access is prohibited.

车辆证照齐全、合法有效且通过年检；

All vehicles shall have complete set of license and pass the annual check.

保险满足要求，包括但不限于：机动车交通事故责任强制保险、机动车损失险、第三者责任险（不低于50万）；

Requirements of insurance, including but not limit to: Compulsory Traffic Accident Liability Insurance for Motor Vehicles, Damage insurance, Third Party Liability Insurance (> 500,000RMB);

乙方租用车辆需保证车龄7年以内或行车里程不超过30万公里。

Party B shall guarantee that the vehicle age is less than 7 years or the driving mileage shall not exceed 300000 km。

车辆所有设施均完好无损；

All facilities are in good condition;

安全配置包含但不限于：安全气囊、防抱死系统（前后）、车载灭火器、常用维修工具、反光三角警示、备胎、行车记录仪（具备自动覆盖功能）； 

Safety equipment, including but not limit to: air bag, ABS, fire extinguisher, common maintenance tools, warning signs, spare tyre, automobile data recorder, etc.

交通运输应遵守以下要求

Traffic transport

严格遵守交通法规安全驾驶。

Obey traffic regulations.

禁止超速、超载、超宽、超长、闯红灯等行为。

No speeding, overloading, ultra-wide, ultra-long, red light running.

行车过程禁止接听或拨打电话、浏览电子设备、与人聊天等行为。

It is forbidden to answer or dial telephone calls, browse electronic equipment and chat with people while driving.

禁止搭乘非指定随车人员，严禁长鸣笛避免噪声污染。

It is forbidden to take non-designated passengers and to whistle strictly.

运输车辆配备相应的消防器材和捆扎、防雨、防散失等用具。

Transport vehicles should be equipped with fire-fighting equipment and bundling, rainproof, loss-proof and other equipment.

进入厂（场）区后必须停靠在指定区域，禁止进入非作业区。

After entering the site, vehicles must be parked in designated areas.

必须听从现场人员的指挥和安排。不得损坏、阻挡或者擅自挪用消防设施，不得占用防火间距，不得堵塞消防通道。

The command and arrangement of the personnel on the spot must be obeyed. No damage, obstruction or unauthorized misappropriation of fire-fighting equipment, no occupancy of fire-fighting spacing, and no blockage of fire-fighting passages.

作业区域内严禁吸烟、明火取暖，不得私拉乱接电气线路或使用大功率电器设备。

Smoking, heating with open fire, power connection without permission or using high-power electrical appliance within the operation area are not allowed.

物流运输服务完成，立即驶离厂（场）区，禁止逗留。

When the transportation service is completed, leave the operation area immediately.

乙方必须编制风机设备运输专项方案，在资源配置计划中上报设备运输车辆的数量、车型。

Party B must formulate special scheme for the transport of wind turbine generator system and report the quantity and type of transport vehicles in resource allocation plan.

乙方运输车辆行驶证符合要求，车辆合格证明、年检报告按要求向监理报验。

Party B’s transport vehicles should have qualified driving licenses, and certificate of vehicle and annual inspection report should be verified by the supervisor.

乙方设备运输车辆进场前应组织对运输路线进行专项检查，按运输方案要求落实措施，确认运输道路具备安全条件。

Party B should organize special inspection to transport routes before its transport vehicles enter the construction site, measurement should be fulfilled strictly according to the transport scheme, and the transport routes should be guaranteed to be safe.

乙方车辆进场前必须安装全球卫星定位系统（GPS ），在场区内限速行驶。

GPS should be installed before Party B’s transport vehicles enter the construction site, and the vehicles should move at limited speed on the construction site. 

乙方设备运输车辆进场前72小时内，将设备进场运输计划报监理和买方审核，并办理风电场设备进场安全检查确认单，得到许可后方可进入。

Party B should report equipment admission and transport plan to the supervisor and the buyer for review 72 hours before its transport vehicles enter the construction site, and the vehicles can enter the construction site only after being approved.

定期对机动车辆检测和检验，保证机动车辆车况良好，严禁在场区内使用报废车辆。

Party B should detect and inspect vehicles periodically, so as to ensure the vehicles are in good condition. End-of-life vehicles are not allowed on the construction site.

确保大件设备运输车辆固定设备用的钢丝、手动葫芦、绑扎带和米字架等符合设备承重安全要求。 

Party B should ensure the wire rope, hand-drive block, binding tape and star-shaped shelf used to fix large equipment for transport are consistent with equipment bearing safety requirement.

严格执行关于道路运输“十不运”管理要求：

Strictly implement “no transportation under below 10 circumstances” road transport management requirements:

车辆和驾驶人员资质不符合要求，车头牵引马力和底盘高度及各项参数不满足合同要求及运输方案不运； 

Do not transport if vehicles and drivers are not qualified or traction horsepower, chassis height, and other parameters do not meet the contract requirements;

车辆驾驶人员、地面引导人员、操作人员身体精神状态不佳、未佩戴“十不运”警示牌、15小时内饮酒不运； 

Do not transport if drivers, ground guiders and operators are not in good condition, or do not wear “no transportation” warning armband, or drink within 15 hours; 

地面引导人员、运输指挥人员、驾驶人员未提前进行道路勘察，不熟悉运输道路情况，无前车引导不运； 

Do not transport if ground guiders, transport commanders and drivers have not made road reconnaissance in advance, or are not familiar with road situation, or have no guided vehicle;

每日在车辆启动前，驾驶人员、地面引导人员、操作人员未参加班前会，未进行“三交三查”不运； 

Do not transport if drivers, ground guiders and operators have not participated pre-shift meeting before starting vehicles, or made “three disclosures and three checks”;

甲方或监理单位未现场监护，监护和引导人员（其他行人）站位不当或距离车辆不足10米不运； 

Do not transport if Party A or the supervision unit does not supervise on site, or guardian or guiders (other pedestrians) do not stand at proper position or stand less than 10m away from vehicles; 

设备运输方案未批准，无道路运输风险辨识与预控措施（方案中未包含叶片运输对风速管控及对山区阵风预控）不运； 

Do not transport if the transport scheme is not approved, or have no road transport risk identification and preventive measures (the scheme does not include wind speed control and montanic gust prevention for blade transport);

运输车辆未安装行车记录仪，未经过全面检查，车辆超载或违规载人、超速，进场前未经过甲方、工程监理签点确认不运； 

Do not transport if transport vehicles have no automobile data recorders, or all-round inspection is not done, or vehicles run with overload, illegal manning, or overspeed, or they are not confirmed by Party A and the supervisor before entering the construction site;

运输车辆配重未经过计算、未可靠固定，塔筒、机舱设备运输缆绳、缆索绑固不满足物流运输工艺要求、车辆载荷重心偏移超安全范围不运； 

Do not transport if the balance weight of vehicle is not calculated or fixed, the tower and engine room facilities are not tied according to the logistic operation and safety requirements, or the centre-of-gravity shift of vehicle is out of safety range;

车辆轮胎与道路软路（土）基距离不足1米不运； 

Do not transport if the tires of vehicles are less than 1m away from soft (earth) subgrade;

运输道路局部区域气候状况不满足运输方案要求，叶片特种工装车举升运输时风速≥8米/秒，设备常规运输时风速≥12米/秒不运。

Do not transport if partial climate condition does not meet the transport requirement, the wind speed is ≥8m/s when hoisting and transporting blade with special trolley, or the wind speed is ≥12m/s during common transportation of equipment.

应急管理应遵守以下要求：

Emergency management

乙方在作业前制定应急准备和响应计划，并对应急计划（预案）的有效性适时进行演练、评审和改进。

The Part B should formulate emergency preparation and response plan before working, and the effectiveness of the plan should be demonstrated, reviewed and improved.

发生事故或紧急情况时，乙方必须立即按应急计划（预案）处理并向甲方报告，采取必要措施防止损害扩大。

If an accident or emergency case occurs, the Part B should start response plan immediately, report to Party A, and take essential measures to prevent the enlarging of damage.

发生事故后，甲方和乙方要在最短时间内寻求外部专业救援机构（如消防队、医院急救）援助和救护，并按法定程序报告政府主管部门。

If an accident occurs, the Part A and the Part B should seek for the aid and rescue of external professional rescue agency (such as fire brigade and hospital emergency center) and report to the competent government department according to required procedure.

自然灾害防范应遵守以下要求：

Natural disaster prevention

根据建设项目的地域特点及自然环境情况，建立自然灾害及事故隐患预测预警管理办法。

Natural disaster and accident potential forecast and warning management method should be established according to the geographical characteristics and natural environment situation where the construction project is located.

因自然灾害可能导致事故的隐患，必须采取预防措施。

Preventive measures should be taken for the accident potential caused by natural disaster.

在接到有关自然灾害预报时，必须及时发出预警通知。

Warning notice should be sent in time after receiving the forecast of natural disaster.

治安监护应遵守以下要求：

Public security management

作业范围装设临时围栏或警告标志，不得超越指定的范围作业，禁止无关人员进入作业现场。

The construction site should be equipped with temporary fence or warning signs. Working out of the designated construction site is not allowed. Irrelevant personnel are not allowed to enter the construction site.

不得擅自使用甲方设施设备，不得擅自拆除、变更甲方防护设施及标示。

The Part A’s facilities, devices and signs should not be used, removed or changed without permission.

安全奖惩

Safety reward and punishment 

事故处罚（见附件三：事故/事件级别定义）

Accident punishment (see Appendix III: Definition of accident/incident level)

一级（重大）事故：由于乙方原因造成的重大事故，事故当事人和直接主管立即清除出场并不得再次进入甲方现场，同时处乙方人民币10万元的罚款处理。

Class I (significant) accident: For significant accidents caused by Party B, the party involved in the accident and the direct supervisor shall be removed from the site immediately and shall not enter the site of Party A again, and Party B shall be fined 100000 yuan.

二级（较重）事故：由于乙方原因造成的较重事故，事故当事人立即清除出场并不得再次进入甲方现场，同时处乙方人民币5万元的罚款处理。

Class II (serious) accident: For serious accident caused by Party B, the party involved in the accident shall be removed from the site immediately and shall not enter the site of Party A again, and Party B shall be fined 50000 yuan.

三级（一般）事故：由于乙方原因造成的一般事故，事故当事人（班组）停工整改，同时处乙方人民币2万元的罚款处理。

Class III (general) accident: For general accidents caused by Party B, the party (team) involved in the accident shall stop work for rectification, and a fine of 20000 yuan shall be imposed on Party B.

EHS红线违规（见附件二：EHS红线）

EHS red line violation (see Appendix II: EHS red line)

由于乙方作业人员违反了甲方EHS红线，当事人立即清除出场并不得再次进入甲方现场，同时处乙方人民币2~5万元/次罚款处理。

As the operators of Party B violate the EHS red line of Party A, the party concerned shall be removed from the site immediately and shall not enter the site of Party A again, and Party B shall be fined 20000~50000 yuan/time.

若乙方在同一自然年度连续发生两起及以上违反EHS红线行为，从第二起，每次扣款5~10万。

If Party B violates the EHS red line twice or more in the same natural year, the second one will be deducted RMB 50000~100000 each time.

若乙方作业人员存在主观恶意违反甲方EHS红线的行为，当事人立即清除出场并不得再次进入甲方现场，同时处乙方人民币5~10万元/次罚款处理。

If the operators of Party B violate the EHS red line of Party A subjectively and maliciously, the party concerned shall be removed from the site immediately and shall not enter the site of Party A again, and Party B shall be fined 50000 to 100000 yuan/time.

非红线违规  Non red line violations

乙方作业人员违反甲方安全管理规定，但未发生事故且未违反EHS红线。按违规内容的严重程度，处乙方人民币0~1万元的罚款处理。

The operators of Party B violate the safety management regulations of Party A, but there is no accident and no violation of the EHS red line. According to the severity of the violation, Party B shall be fined within 10000 yuan.

减罚原则  Penalty reduction principle

乙方在甲方现场发生上述的事故或违反EHS红线/一般违规，凡能积极配合调查，认真整改且被认为有效的，甲方可酌情在原罚款额度上减免10%~30%。

If Party B has the above accidents or violates the EHS red line/general violations at Party A's site, and can actively cooperate with the investigation, seriously rectify and be considered effective, Party A may, at its discretion, reduce or exempt 10%~30% of the original fine.

虽是乙方人员，但管理权属在甲方的，除对当事人按上述条款处罚外，不对乙方做处罚处理。

If the management authority belongs to Party A even though he/she is Party B's personnel, Party B will not be punished except for the punishment according to the above provisions.

6、甲方将本着客观、公正的原则，定时对乙方在所有项目现场进行打KPI分，在评审周期结束的时候对供应商KPI打分汇总分析进行排名，排名分成三档分别为绿色，黄色和红色。其中80分（含）以上的为绿色，说明此供应商服务水平较好；60分（含）到80分（不含） 为黄色，说明此供应商服务水平及格，但还需改进；60分（不含）以下说明该供应商不合格；连续两年不合格，将列入远景供应商黑名单不与合作。

Party A will, based on the principle of objectivity and impartiality, regularly score Party B's KPIs at all project sites, and rank the suppliers' KPI scoring summary analysis at the end of the review cycle.
